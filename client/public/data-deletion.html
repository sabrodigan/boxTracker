User Data Deletion Instructions

BoxTracker respects your privacy. If you have logged into BoxTracker using Facebook and would like to delete your account and all associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: Remove via Facebook

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Scroll down and click Apps and Websites.
  4. Find "BoxTracker" in the list of active apps and click Remove.
  5. Once removed, Facebook will notify us, and we will purge your account and associated storage box data from our servers within 30 days.

Option 2: Direct Request

You can request immediate deletion of your BoxTracker account and all your associated boxes/items by contacting the administrator directly. Your data will be permanently erased from our database.
