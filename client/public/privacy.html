Privacy Policy for BoxTracker

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you log in to BoxTracker using social accounts (Google or Facebook), we collect your email address, name, and a unique identifier associated with your account. We use this information solely to create and authenticate your BoxTracker account.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to provide the BoxTracker service to you, allowing you to save your boxes and items securely. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored on our cloud infrastructure. Passwords (if created manually) are hashed and encrypted.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the site administrator.
